The Process

How AutoGreet Works

Four simple steps — and you don't need to do any of them. We handle the entire setup for you.

1

Customer Visits

A customer visits your business or receives your service. You upload their name and WhatsApp number via our simple portal.

2

WhatsApp Request Sent

2–4 hours later, a personalised WhatsApp message is automatically sent asking them for a Google review — with a one-click link.

3

AI Responds to Reviews

Every new Google review — positive or negative — gets a thoughtful, on-brand AI response posted within 2 hours. 24/7.

4

Your Reputation Grows

Your Google rating climbs. More reviews. More trust. More customers choosing you over the competition.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any technical knowledge?
None at all. We set everything up for you — 100% done-for-you. You simply keep serving your customers.
How do customers get the WhatsApp message?
You (or your staff) upload the customer's name and phone number via our simple portal. AutoGreet handles the rest — the message goes out automatically 2–4 hours later.
What if I get a negative review?
AutoGreet instantly alerts you and posts a professional, empathetic response on your behalf — turning a difficult moment into a trust-building opportunity.
How soon will I see results?
Most clients see their first new reviews within 48–72 hours of their setup going live.
Can I customise the messages?
Yes — on Growth and Authority plans, we train the AI on your brand voice so every message and review response sounds like it came from you personally.
Is there a contract or lock-in?
No long-term contracts. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime, no questions asked.
