How AutoGreet Works

1

Customer Visits

A customer visits your restaurant, salon, or clinic. Your staff uploads their name + WhatsApp number to our simple dashboard.

2

Auto WhatsApp Message

3–4 hours later, the customer receives a warm, personalised WhatsApp message from your business asking for a Google review.

3

One-Tap Review Link

The message includes a direct link to your Google review page. One tap — and they're writing a review. No searching needed.

4

AI Responds Automatically

Every new Google review gets an AI-generated, professional reply instantly — saving you hours and impressing customers.

5

Follow-Up If No Response

If the customer didn't click the link, a polite follow-up message is sent 24 hours later — doubling your response rate.

6

Track Everything

Your client dashboard shows all messages sent, reviews collected, and your rating progress — updated in real time.
